Puzzle Tap is a fresh take on jigsaw puzzles. Tap slots to draw pieces from your deck and place them on the board — but only if a neighbor is already placed. Plan ahead, manage your hand, and complete each puzzle one tap at a time.

Features:
• Slot-based piece drawing — manage your hand strategically
• Neighbor-adjacency rules — every placement counts
• Beautifully illustrated environments to restore
• Relaxing gameplay with no time pressure
• New puzzles added regularly
